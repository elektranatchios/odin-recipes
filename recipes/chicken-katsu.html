Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken Katsu is a classic Japanese home-cooked meal served with tonkatsu sauce and a side of shredded cabbage salad.

It is a chicken fillet breaded with flour, egg, and Japanese panko breadcrumbs, then deep-fried until golden brown. It's the chicken counterpart of Tonkatsu or pork cutlet

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko (Japanese Breadcrumbs)
  • Tonkatsu Sauce

Procedure

  1. Butterfly the Chicken Breast
    2. We butterfly the chicken breast by splitting it horizontally, stopping before you cut all the way through it, and opening it like a book. When you open the breast it has two matching sides, resembling a butterfly.
  2. Coat the chicken with flour, egg, and panko.
  3. deep fry until golden brown and serve with tonkatsu sauce.
